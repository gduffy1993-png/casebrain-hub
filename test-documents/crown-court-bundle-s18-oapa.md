## CHARGE SHEET

**Charge 1:**  
On 23 November 2023 at Leeds, West Yorkshire, unlawfully and maliciously wounded Marcus Johnson with intent to do him grievous bodily harm, contrary to section 18 of the Offences Against the Person Act 1861.

**Particulars of Offence:**  
Daniel James Hughes, on the 23rd day of November 2023, at the premises known as "Golden Dragon Takeaway", 89 Kirkstall Road, Leeds, did unlawfully and maliciously wound Marcus Johnson, intending to cause him grievous bodily harm, by stabbing him with a knife.

**Maximum Penalty:** Life imprisonment

---

## PROSECUTION OPENING CASE SUMMARY

The prosecution case is that on the evening of 23 November 2023, at approximately 23:45 hours, the defendant Daniel Hughes entered the Golden Dragon Takeaway on Kirkstall Road, Leeds, and became involved in an altercation with the complainant Marcus Johnson. During this altercation, the defendant produced a knife and stabbed Mr Johnson in the abdomen, causing a serious penetrating injury requiring emergency surgery.

The prosecution will rely upon:
1. The evidence of the complainant Marcus Johnson, who identifies the defendant as his assailant
2. The evidence of independent witnesses who observed the incident
3. CCTV footage from the takeaway premises
4. Medical evidence demonstrating the serious nature of the injury
5. Forensic evidence linking the defendant to the scene
6. The defendant's subsequent arrest and interview

The prosecution contends that the defendant acted with intent to cause grievous bodily harm, as evidenced by the use of a knife, the location of the injury, and the force used.

## WITNESS STATEMENT: MARCUS JOHNSON (COMPLAINANT)

**Statement Date:** 25 November 2023

**Witness DOB:** 8 July 1995  
**Address:** 12 Oakwood Drive, Headingley, Leeds, LS6 3HJ  
**Occupation:** Retail Assistant

**Statement:**

My name is Marcus Johnson. I am 28 years old and I work as a retail assistant at a shop in the city centre. On the evening of 23 November 2023, I went to the Golden Dragon Takeaway on Kirkstall Road with my friends Liam and Ryan. We got there at about half past ten, maybe a bit later. We ordered some food and waited for it to be ready.

We were eating inside the takeaway when this bloke came in. I didn't really pay much attention to him at first. He was talking to the staff, seemed a bit annoyed about something. I think he left and came back in later. I'm not sure of the exact times, it all happened quite fast.

I went to the toilet at the back of the takeaway. When I came back, my friends were getting ready to leave. As I was walking towards the door, this bloke came back in. He was the same one from before. He started saying something to me, I can't remember exactly what. I think he said something about me looking at him or something. I told him to calm down, I wasn't looking for any trouble.

He got really aggressive then. He was swearing and getting in my face. I tried to back away but he followed me. Then he pulled out a knife. I saw it clearly, it was a kitchen knife, looked quite big. I panicked and tried to get away but he lunged at me and stabbed me in the stomach.

I felt this sharp pain and I fell to the floor. I could feel blood. It was really scary. People were helping me, putting pressure on the wound. Someone called an ambulance. The bloke ran off straight away.

I was taken to hospital in an ambulance. I had to have surgery. The doctors said it was serious. I was in hospital for about a week.

I don't know the bloke's name. I've never seen him before that night. But I would recognise him if I saw him again. He was white, about 5 foot 10 or 11, medium build, dark hair, wearing a dark hooded top. He looked like he was in his early thirties.

When the police showed me some photos later, I picked out Daniel Hughes. I'm sure it was him. I'm certain it was the same person who stabbed me.

I want to make a complaint about this. I didn't do anything to provoke him. I was just trying to leave the takeaway and he attacked me for no reason.

**Signed:** Marcus Johnson
