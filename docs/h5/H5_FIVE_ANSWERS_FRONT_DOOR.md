## 1. Default first screen — Five Answers / Evidence Truth View

Replace the current first impression with five solicitor questions:

1. **What is this case saying?**
2. **What is served / referred only / missing?**
3. **What must I not overstate?**
4. **What do I chase?**
5. **What source-backed court note can solicitor review?**

Presentation layer only — data from existing modules 1–7, Guardian, source-state, chase builder.

---

## 2. Evidence Trace — two axes

| Axis | Values |
|------|--------|
| **Existence** | served · referred_only · missing · unknown · not_safely_confirmed |
| **Reliability** | strong · weak · contested · unsafe · inference_only · needs_review |

Click-through from any Five Answers line → trace with both axes visible.

---

## 3. Safer UI language

| Old (avoid) | New (firm-facing) |
|-------------|-------------------|
| Safe court line | **Source-backed court note** |
| Safe to send (unqualified) | **Copy suggestion** |
| — | **Solicitor review required** |
| — | **Not for sending until reviewed** |

Aligns with H3 sendability labels; no weakening of Guardian gates.

---

## 4. Contradictions

- **Do not** build new contradiction detection.
- Surface **existing** modules 1–7 inside Evidence Trace, Defence Decision Board, Advice Radar, Hearing Mode.

---

## 5. Hard rules (non-negotiable)

- **Served** does not mean **reliable**.
- **Missing** does not mean **irrelevant**.
- **Referred only** does not mean **usable**.
- **Inference** must be labelled as inference.
