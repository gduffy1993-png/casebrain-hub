# Stress-test case: what we need

One full criminal case bundle so you can test the app end-to-end. All below in **one document** (then save as PDF) or split into multiple PDFs — your choice.

---

## 1. Charge sheet

- Court name, case number, date of charge
- Defendant: full name, DOB, address (made up)
- Offence: exact wording + section (e.g. s.18 OAPA 1861 or s.9(1)(a) Theft Act 1968)
- Date of offence, brief allegation line

---

## 2. MG5 (Case summary)

- 1–2 pages: what the prosecution say happened, key evidence, defendant’s account (e.g. self-defence or no comment)

---

## 3. MG11 (Witness statements)

- **Complainant** – what they say happened, injuries, ID of defendant
- **Police officer** – attendance, arrest, BWV/CCTV
- **One other** – e.g. bystander or custody officer (short)

---

## 4. Medical report

- Doctor/hospital, date of examination
- Injuries (e.g. laceration, bruising, fracture)
- Mechanism (single blow vs multiple, weapon vs fist)
- Opinion on intent if relevant (for GBH)

---

## 5. CCTV / BWV summary

- What’s on the footage, times, gaps
- Whether defendant identifiable, what the footage shows (e.g. “blow to head”, “punch”)

---

## 6. Custody record

- Date/time in custody, PACE clock, next review
- Interview: time, “no comment” or “answered”
- Solicitor attendance

---

## 7. Short disclosure list (optional)

- 5–10 items (CCTV, BWV, medical, MG11s, unused material) so the app sees “disclosure” content

---

## Test case we’ll use

- **Offence:** Wounding with intent (s.18 OAPA 1861) — so the app should show GBH/assault strategy (charge reduction s18→s20, intent, causation).
- **Scenario:** Pub car park, one punch, complainant falls and hits head. Defendant says self-defence.
- **Defendant (fake):** Jordan Michael WRIGHT, DOB 15/03/1992, address in Manchester.
- **Dates:** Offence 14/09/2024, arrest same night, charge 15/09/2024.
- **Location:** Fictional “The Crown” pub, Manchester; custody at “Central Park” custody suite.

All names, DOBs, addresses and dates are made up for testing only.
