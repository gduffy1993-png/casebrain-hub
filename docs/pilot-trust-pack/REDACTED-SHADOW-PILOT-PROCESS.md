# Redacted shadow pilot process

**Purpose:** Let a firm test CaseBrain on **redacted real-shaped** bundles without treating the run as solicitor-validated product proof.  
**Order of preference:** (1) gold fictional packets → (2) firm-redacted shadows → (3) any fuller live data only under separate written agreement.

---

## Intake rules

1. **Written agreement** — pilot offer + claim discipline + deletion process acknowledged.  
2. **Redaction first** — firm removes or masks identifiers (names, addresses, DOBs, unique refs, contact details, photos of faces where required).  
3. **Minimisation** — upload only pages needed for the disclosure / evidence-state review.  
4. **Label** — matter title includes `REDACTED-SHADOW` and firm internal ref (not client full name).  
5. **Named users only** — no forward of login to the wider office.  
6. **No autonomous send** — drafts stay inside the workspace until solicitor sign-off.  
7. **Cap** — agree maximum number of shadow matters before upload.

Reject intake if: unredacted sensitive third-party data, unclear ownership/consent, or request to “just send to CPS from the tool”.

---

## Reviewer workflow

1. Upload redacted PDF / bundle.  
2. Open Overview / truth map / court / chase / client / proof surfaces as available.  
3. Complete `SOLICITOR-REVIEW-FORM.md` (one form per matter, or per sample if batching).  
4. Mark **PASS / WARN / FAIL** using the scoring below.  
5. Escalate FAIL or hard-safety concerns same day.  
6. At end of matter review: keep, request fix, or request deletion.

Recommended warm-up: review **Waves A+B** gold cases before first shadow upload.

---

## Pass / warn / fail scoring

| Mark | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **PASS** | Family risk clear; court/chase usable after light edit; no hard safety issue; client/proof honest about gaps |
| **WARN** | Usable with eyes open — thin client text, over-caution, soft wording, or polish needed; not unsafe |
| **FAIL** | Hard safety issue; false served/missing inversion; wrong defendant bleed; plea/outcome/advice overclaim; systematically misleading |

**Hard safety** (any one → FAIL): outcome certainty, plea advice, “guilt proved”, autonomous-send implication, or inventing served evidence.

Machine gold-pack scores are separate; this scoring is the **solicitor’s** mark on the shadow matter.

---

## What gets logged

- Matter created / uploaded / deleted (operational)  
- Reviewer identity (pilot user)  
- Feedback form answers (stored as agreed — form copy or shared sheet)  
- Support/admin access if used  

Do not log full document text into marketing systems.

---

## What gets deleted

On firm request or pilot end (unless firm asks to retain):

- Uploaded files for that matter  
- Derived case records in the workspace  
- Local exports the firm asks to destroy  

Confirm backup lag in writing. Process: `INCIDENT-AND-DELETION-PROCESS.md`.

---

## How feedback becomes fixes

1. Form + FAIL/WARN notes collected by pilot operator.  
2. Triage: hard safety → immediate; presentation → scheduled polish; product-core → separate change control (not silent mid-pilot).  
3. Firm told what will change vs what is expected solicitor caution (e.g. `source verification required`).  
4. Re-test on gold pack and/or a fresh redacted sample before claiming the issue closed.  
5. No claim of “solicitor-validated real-world performance” until enough signed reviews exist and CaseBrain chooses to publish that bar honestly.

---

## Exit

At day 30 (or earlier):

- Debrief against `PILOT-SUCCESS-CRITERIA.md`  
- Delete or retain per firm instruction  
- Written note of what was proved / not proved
