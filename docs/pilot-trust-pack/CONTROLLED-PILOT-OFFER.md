# Controlled pilot offer — 30 days

**Product:** CaseBrain (criminal disclosure / evidence-state review aid)  
**Offer type:** Supervised pilot — not a production rollout  
**Proof baseline:** Controlled fictional scale + gold manual pack PASS; not solicitor-validated on live files yet

---

## Offer in one paragraph

CaseBrain invites a limited number of criminal firms to a **30-day supervised pilot**. Users review controlled gold packets (Waves A+B or full 20) and/or a small number of **firm-redacted** matters. Outputs are drafts for solicitor review only. Nothing is sent to court or CPS without human sign-off. Success is measured by gaps spotted, unsafe assumptions blocked, usable chase/court wording after edit, and time saved — not by autonomous accuracy claims.

---

## Scope limits

| Limit | Rule |
|-------|------|
| Duration | 30 days from kick-off (extend only by written agreement) |
| Users | Named solicitors / fee-earners only (small set; typically 1–5) |
| Matters | Limited count agreed in writing (e.g. gold pack review ± a few redacted shadows) |
| Material first | Prefer **fictional / gold** packets first; then **redacted** firm bundles |
| Live unredacted client data | Not in default offer — only if separately agreed after redaction process |
| Sending | **No autonomous sending** to court, CPS, client, or opposing parties |
| Legal responsibility | Remains with the instructing solicitor / firm |

---

## What participants get

1. Access to CaseBrain for agreed users and matters  
2. Gold Manual Proof Set review path (Waves A+B recommended start)  
3. Optional redacted shadow intake under `REDACTED-SHADOW-PILOT-PROCESS.md`  
4. Feedback forms and a short debrief  
5. Deletion on request under `INCIDENT-AND-DELETION-PROCESS.md`

---

## What participants must do

1. Use CaseBrain as a **supervised review aid** only  
2. Sign off (or reject) any court note, CPS chase, or client wording before use  
3. Not treat outputs as legal advice or plea/outcome guidance  
4. Keep pilot material confidential; do not upload unredacted third-party data without agreement  
5. Complete the solicitor review form for sampled matters  
6. Flag any unsafe or misleading output immediately

---

## Solicitor sign-off required

Before any external use of CaseBrain wording:

- [ ] Solicitor has read the relevant output  
- [ ] Source / evidence state checked where material  
- [ ] Unsafe-to-say / do-not-overstate respected  
- [ ] Firm decides whether to use, edit, or discard  

CaseBrain does **not** replace professional judgment.

---

## Success criteria (summary)

Full detail: `PILOT-SUCCESS-CRITERIA.md`.

At a minimum, the pilot is a success if, on agreed sample matters:

- Material disclosure gaps are spotted that a busy fee-earner might miss  
- Unsafe assumptions (served vs referred vs missing) are blocked or caveated  
- CPS chase drafts are accepted or lightly edited  
- Court notes are usable after solicitor review  
- Client summaries are clearer than the raw papers alone (where used)  
- Time is saved on first-pass disclosure triage  
- False positives / over-warning are tracked honestly  

Failure modes that stop promotion: hard safety issues (plea/outcome/advice overclaim), systematic false “served”, or loss of solicitor trust.

---

## What we will not claim from this pilot alone

- Guaranteed accuracy on all real files  
- Solicitor-validated real-world performance industry-wide  
- SOC 2 / ISO / pen-test / SRA endorsement  
- Ready for unsupervised firm-wide rollout
