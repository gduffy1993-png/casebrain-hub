## üîç **System Components**

### **1. Loophole Detection Engine**

#### **A. Legal Technicalities Finder**
- **Statute of Limitations** - Is charge time-barred?
- **Double Jeopardy** - Has client been tried for this before?
- **Jurisdiction Issues** - Wrong court/venue?
- **Charge Duplication** - Same facts, multiple charges?
- **Sentencing Errors** - Wrong sentencing guidelines applied?
- **Mandatory Minimums** - Can they be avoided?

#### **B. Procedural Loopholes**
- **PACE Breaches:**
  - Caution not given properly
  - Interview not recorded
  - Right to solicitor denied
  - Detention time exceeded
  - Search without warrant/proper authority
  - Evidence not properly seized
  
- **Disclosure Failures:**
  - Incomplete disclosure
  - Late disclosure
  - Unused material not disclosed
  - Expert reports not disclosed
  - Witness statements missing
  
- **Court Procedure Errors:**
  - Wrong court (summary vs. indictable)
  - Charge not properly drafted
  - Indictment defects
  - Service errors
  - Time limits breached

#### **C. Evidence Exclusion Opportunities**
- **Hearsay** - Can evidence be excluded?
- **Bad Character** - Can previous convictions be excluded?
- **Identification** - Can ID evidence be excluded?
- **Confession** - Can confession be excluded (oppression)?
- **Forensic** - Chain of custody issues?
- **Expert** - Expert not properly qualified?

### **Strategy 4: Contradictory Evidence ‚Üí Unreliable ‚Üí Case Dismissed**

**How It Works:**
1. Find contradictions in prosecution evidence
2. Amplify contradictions
3. Argue evidence unreliable
4. Request case dismissed

**Example:**

### **Strategy 5: Missing Evidence ‚Üí Benefit of Doubt ‚Üí Acquittal**

**How It Works:**
1. Identify missing evidence that could help defense
2. Argue prosecution failed to investigate
3. Request benefit of doubt
4. Argue case not proven beyond reasonable doubt

**Example:**
```
MISSING EVIDENCE:
- No CCTV from alibi location

- Forensic evidence not collected

ARGUMENT:
"The prosecution has failed to investigate evidence that
could prove my client's innocence. The missing CCTV could
show my client was elsewhere. The phone records could
confirm the alibi. Without this evidence, there is
reasonable doubt. I submit the case is not proven."

SUCCESS RATE: 50% (depends on strength of alibi)
```
