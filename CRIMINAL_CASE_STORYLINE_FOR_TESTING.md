## üìÖ **TIMELINE OF EVENTS**

### **12th January 2024 - The Incident**

**2:30pm** - Defendant James Mitchell enters TechZone Electronics store on High Street, Manchester. CCTV shows him browsing mobile phones.

**2:35pm** - Store manager Sarah Williams notices Mitchell acting suspiciously near the iPhone display. She alerts security guard Michael Brown.

**2:40pm** - Security guard Michael Brown approaches Mitchell. According to Brown's statement, Mitchell was holding an iPhone 15 Pro (value ¬£1,250) and attempting to leave without paying.

**2:41pm** - Confrontation occurs. Brown states Mitchell pushed him, causing him to fall and sustain minor injuries (bruising to left arm). Mitchell denies this.

**2:42pm** - Police called. Store staff detain Mitchell until police arrive.

**3:15pm** - Police arrive (PC David Harris and PC Emma Wilson). Mitchell arrested on suspicion of theft and assault.

**3:20pm** - Mitchell taken to Manchester Central Police Station.
