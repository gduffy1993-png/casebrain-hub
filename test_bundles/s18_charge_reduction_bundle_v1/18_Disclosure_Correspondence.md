## Letter 1: Defence Request (20 August 2024)
**From:** Defence Solicitors (fictional firm)

Dear Sir/Madam,

Re: CRN-TEST-8742 | URN-23-TEST-4411 | R v Jordan Clarke

We act for the defendant. We acknowledge receipt of the MG6C schedule dated 17 August 2024.

We note that the schedule is incomplete. We request:

1. **Complete MG6C schedule** - Please provide a completed schedule with all unused material items listed and their disclosure status.

2. **CCTV Continuity** - Please provide full continuity documentation for CCTV exhibits (EXH/002, EXH/003, EXH/004), including:

6. **Timetable** - Please provide a timetable for completion of outstanding disclosure items.

We look forward to your response.

Yours faithfully,  
Defence Solicitors
